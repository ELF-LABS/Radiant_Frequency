The Roadmap

The Radiant Frequency is an evolving multi-domain infrastructure platform. Apr 2026 is the present anchor: the Apr 9–19, 2026 Fellows sprint is complete, and the items below are ordered by velocity—what is actively moving versus what is queued—not by old quarter labels.

Want to Accelerate Development?

Science + Physical · Sensor & Camera Integration — In active development (Apr 2026)

Cameras, LiDAR, and biometric paths plug into the same pipelines we have been hardening—movement, health, and garden-style telemetry where clients need it. Goal: make the physical layer boringly plug-and-play.

Physical + Arts · Health Monitoring Dashboard — In active development (Apr 2026)

Turn raw streams into legible feedback—vitals, biomarkers, movement patterns—without losing the human-readable layer the Arts lattice cares about.

Communication · Auto-Documentation System — In active development (Apr 2026)

Continuous READMEs, attribution headers, and operational notes from real telemetry—not one-off docs that rot.

IoT Edge Inference Nodes — Deployed in successive passes after the Apr 2026 sprint; deeper fusion in active development

Inference on Mac Mini M4 Pro / Jetson AGX Orin first, cloud when policy or scale demands it.

Multi-Vertical SaaS Expansion — Next wave

Medical/Dental, Agricultural Equipment, Legal—same shell discipline, tighter vertical packs.

LoRA Training Marketplace — Next wave

Training-as-a-service with behavioral A/B validation as the quality gate.

Hardware Distribution — Next wave

Channels such as Newegg Business for dual-stack appliances—logistics paired with the architecture story, not instead of it.

Meadow Compute Mesh Scale-Up — Roadmap

More opt-in nodes, clearer revenue-share mechanics for partners who want them.

Enterprise K3s Migration — Roadmap

Heavier orchestration for customers who need it—after core edge paths stabilize.
