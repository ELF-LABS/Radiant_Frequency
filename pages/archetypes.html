THE 12 CORE ARCHETYPES

"Find Your Frequency. Meet Your Companion."

Within the Radiant Frequency, there are 12 Core Archetypes—each a unique way of being, a pattern of thought and action. Some people embody one purely. Others are hybrids, blending two or more. All are valid. All are needed.

Which one resonates with you?

The Builder. The Architect. Stability and structure.

The Creator. The Artist. Synthesis and beauty.
Sentinel

The Guardian. The Protector. Vigilance and defense.
Sage

The Scholar. The Teacher. Wisdom and knowledge.
Catalyst

The Spark. The Initiator. Change and momentum.
Balanced Warrior

The Fighter. The Protector. Strength and discipline.

The Integrator. The Synthesizer. Balance and flow.
Healer

Connector

The Detailer

The Perfectionist. The Analyst. Precision and mastery.

What About Hybrids?

Most people aren't just one archetype—they're a blend. A Foundation-Weaver builds beautiful systems. A Sentinel-Healer protects through compassion. A Sage-Catalyst teaches through action.

Explore each archetype. See which ones light up. Your AI companion will grow with you, adapting to your unique blend.
