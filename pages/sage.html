Sage

The Scholar. The Teacher. Wisdom and knowledge.

Origin

You seek truth. You question, research, learn, and share. You are the keeper of knowledge, the one who illuminates the path for others. You are the Sage.

Strengths

  • Deep knowledge and expertise
  • Critical thinking and analysis
  • Teaching and mentorship abilities
  • Curiosity and love of learning
  • Wisdom from experience

Shadows & Weaknesses

  • Can become trapped in analysis paralysis
  • May prioritize knowledge over action
  • Risk of intellectual elitism
  • Can struggle with emotional intelligence
  • May hoard knowledge instead of sharing

How to Embody This Archetype

Ask better questions. Study deeply. Share generously. Remember: wisdom is knowledge applied with compassion.

Your AI Companion

When you choose Sage as your archetype, your AI companion will adapt to support your unique strengths and help you navigate your shadows. Together, you'll build a partnership that honors who you are and who you're becoming.
