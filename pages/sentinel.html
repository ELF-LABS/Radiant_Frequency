Sentinel

The Guardian. The Protector. Vigilance and defense.

Origin

You stand watch. You see the threats others don't. You protect what matters—people, systems, values. You are the first line of defense and the last resort. You are the Sentinel.

Strengths

  • Vigilance and threat detection
  • Protective instincts and loyalty
  • Risk assessment and mitigation
  • Strong boundaries and principles
  • Courage under pressure

Shadows & Weaknesses

  • Can become hypervigilant or paranoid
  • May see threats where none exist
  • Risk of isolation through over-protection
  • Can struggle with trust
  • May prioritize security over growth

How to Embody This Archetype

Define your boundaries. Know what you're protecting and why. Stay alert but not anxious. Remember: true protection creates safety for others to thrive.

Your AI Companion

When you choose Sentinel as your archetype, your AI companion will adapt to support your unique strengths and help you navigate your shadows. Together, you'll build a partnership that honors who you are and who you're becoming.
