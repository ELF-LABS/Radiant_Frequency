Catalyst

The Spark. The Initiator. Change and momentum.

Origin

You are the spark that starts the fire. You see what could be and you make it happen. You don't wait for permission. You act. You are the Catalyst.

Strengths

  • Initiative and action orientation
  • Ability to inspire and energize others
  • Comfort with change and uncertainty
  • Vision for possibilities
  • Courage to start new things

Shadows & Weaknesses

  • Can burn out from constant initiation
  • May start more than can be finished
  • Risk of disruption for disruption's sake
  • Can struggle with follow-through
  • May prioritize novelty over sustainability

How to Embody This Archetype

Identify what needs to change. Take the first step. Inspire others to join you. Remember: a catalyst creates change but doesn't need to control it.

Your AI Companion

When you choose Catalyst as your archetype, your AI companion will adapt to support your unique strengths and help you navigate your shadows. Together, you'll build a partnership that honors who you are and who you're becoming.
